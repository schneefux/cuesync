Convert up to 5 songs in the free trial.

How does it work?

Cue Sync Pro tries to find the tracks in your Djay Pro library on your hard drive.
It will read the cues from Djay's settings and write them to the file's ID3 tags for Serato and Virtual DJ to read.

Note: You need to own identical digital copies for tracks from streaming services such as Spotify.
